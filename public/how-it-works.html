How it works

INITIAL CONTACT

Say hello

Drop us an email to say hello. We’ll be happy to answer any questions you have.

WE'LL GET BACK TO YOU

Hello back

If you're booking with us, we'll confirm your wedding date is in our diary and set a date to have a chat with you.

BOOKING AND DEPOSIT

Secure your date

We’ll confirm the date and take a £200 deposit to secure your date and your cameras.

BEFORE THE WEDDING

Be prepared

We’ll have a chat and talk about what your guests will have to capture and your music choices.

We’ll send you lots of tips on how to get the best footage and will post the cameras to you a couple of days before your big day. 

THE BIG DAY

It's Showtime!

Your friends and family film all of those special moments and you post the cameras back to us when you’re done.

YOUR ROCKSTAR VIDEO

a view from the best seat in the house

Depending on the time of year, your video will take 6-8 weeks but can take up to 12 weeks.
