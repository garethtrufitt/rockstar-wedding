About Rockstar Wedding

  • Hi there, welcome to Rockstar Wedding!

    If you're looking for a wedding video with a little personality, you're in the right place.

    We make fun, alternative wedding videos that are packed with laughs, personality and ace music that reflects you as a couple and your wedding day!

    Why are they so personal? Well, because we don’t film them. You do!

    We found that people are most likely to be silly, show off their best (or worst) dance moves and leave lovely messages for you if they’re relaxed and what better way to put everyone at ease than to get your own friends and family to capture the best moments? Plus, they know who the important people are and are there for all the best moments. So, relax, enjoy yourself and let your wedding be captured from the best seat in the house!

    You’ll have load of input before, we’ll speak to you about the music you like, get an idea of you as a couple.

    We’ll send you some broadcast quality 4K cameras and give you lots of advice on how and when to use them. Then we’ll work our editing magic on your footage and turn them into an awesome wedding video!

    Take a look around and if you like what you see, drop us an email, we'd love to chat to you.

A bit about the people behind Rockstar Wedding

  • After struggling to find a company that would make us a wedding video that was going to reflect us as a couple, we decided to do it ourselves!

    We both work in creative industries (Hayley is a Creative TV Producer and Gareth is an Editorial Engineering Manager for a major publisher) so we had plenty of friends who would happily pick up a camera and shoot away.

    We didn’t think it would turn out so well. We had so much fun watching it back and editing it. It perfectly reflected just how much fun we had on the day and we realised that our friends had captured the most wonderful moments. Ones that only friends can capture of other friends.

    We got a fantastic response to our video, obviously from our friends (who doesn’t like to watch back their best dance moves from the party!) but also from people we’ve never met before!

    We did another video, and then another and it just went from there. Rockstar Wedding was born.
