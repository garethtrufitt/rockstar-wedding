INITIAL CONTACT

Drop us an email to say hello. We’ll be happy to answer any questions you have.

If you've decided we're what you want, it would be great if you could include this info: your names, date of your wedding, the package you’re interested in and anything else you’d like to ask us!

WE'LL GET BACK TO YOU

If you're booking with us, we'll confirm your wedding date is in our diary and set a date to have a chat with you.

DEPOSIT AND PAYMENT

We'll take a £200 deposit to secure your date and cameras (which is refunded once the cameras are sent back).
Payment is due in full, one month before your wedding date.

BEFORE THE WEDDING

We’ll have a chat and talk about what your guests will have to capture and your music choices.

We’ll send you lots of tips on how to get the best footage and will post the cameras to you a couple of days before your big day.

THE BIG DAY

Your friends and family film all of those special moments and you post the cameras back to us when you’re done.

YOUR ROCKSTAR WEDDING VIDEO

Your video will be with you in typically 6-8 weeks but, depending on the time of year, can take up to 12 weeks.
